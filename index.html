Detect toxic behaviors

Break after loss trade

You appear to emotionally re-enter the market very quick after closing a loss trade.

Our solution

You trade quickly after a loss trade show a significantly negative performance. If you take the time to analyze the market, the trades show a profitable result on average.

Improve trade timing

Cutting profits early

You tend to manually close your profitable trades before they reach your Take Profit level.

Our solution

If You would have done nothing during your profitable trades, just let your trades go into the Take Profit or Stop Loss, You would have made significantly more profit.

Warn of excessive risk

Doubling-down

You do not early cut losing trades early, but adds additional positions to it.

Our solution

You would have higher profits if not applying a DCA (dollar-cost-average) strategy. Adding positions to a loss trade shows a negative result on average for you.

Our solution
