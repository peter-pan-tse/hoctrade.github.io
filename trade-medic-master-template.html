You showed a strong performance whenever your trade matched the short-term trend at trade entry. In total, you made a profit of 6,054 USD in these trades,. Keep building on this strength and continue riding such short-term trend environments.

Explanation

Catching Short-term Trends refers to a trader's ability to profit from trades that align with short-term market trends, while disregarding longer-term trends.

This strength is particularly valuable for traders focused on capturing short-term price movements, such as scalpers or day traders. To succeed, the trader must identify and enter the short-term trend early enough to capture enough of the price movement before it fades, while also being able to recognize early reversal signals that indicate a potential shift. Traders with this strength can profit regardless of the longer-term market trend.

Risk message

Explanation

Increase risk in loss streaks describes the behavior of increasing your risk per trade after incurring a number of losses in your previous trades.

Some traders perceive the winning likelihood of their trades higher in case the had a couple of loss trades beforehand. This behavior roots in a human behavioral tendency described as the Gambler's Fallacy. Moreover, some traders tend to revenge trade after losses combined with higher risks per trade.
