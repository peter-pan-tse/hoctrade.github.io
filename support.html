Analytic related
The benchmark will be defined based on the profitable traders listed on hoc-trade that show a similar trading style compared to yours. You will see the overall performance in the different categories of those traders. The benchmark will be updated on a regular basis based on the trading performance of the traders in your benchmark group and will not be manually adjusted from hoc-trade.
For most of the charts, you can choose whether to present the result in pips or return. For pips, we will calculate the pip value based on the price difference and the point multiplier as presented by your broker. For the calculation of return, we will calculate the return based on your profit of that specific trade and your balance at the time of entering the trade. You will be presented the average performance per trade in your output chart.
In order to help you judging, whether the presented outputs show a level of significance, we will present you with the number of trades underlying the calculation of the presented value. The higher the number of trades, in general the higher the reliability of the output. Additionally, the color of the bars presented in the charts may differ depending on how many trades are underlying to that result. However, please note that we do not apply a calculation for the statistical significance of the output presented. You should always keep in mind, that prior performance may not represent future results and you will use that information at your own risk. We will and cannot take any responsibility for your performance if using that information for your trading.
The different colors in the chart are meant to support you with the interpretation of the charts. The color may vary based on the value presented as well as the number of underlying trades to calculate that value.
We will store your data in GMT time zone, however transfer the data output to your time zone depending on where you access our tool. For classifying your trades into a specific date, we will however use GMT time zone. Most of the analyses are based on the opening time of your trade, however for some analyses we may also choose the closing time of your trade in case feel this is better applicable for the specific analysis.
When presenting the overall return of a trading account, we will use the time-weighted rate of return (TWR) in our calculation. By using the TWR, the timing of deposits or withdrawals from your account do not have an influence on the performance presented.
In case you have further questions or problems understanding the analyses, please also visit the explanation videos related to each analysis. We will have an in-depth explanation on the background, the structure, and potential interpretation of each analysis.
Functionalities
The bookmark function allows you to have the chart listed in your customized tab in your hoc-trade analytics. Once the bookmark is activated, the chart will be shown in that customized tab, and if the bookmark is de-selected, the chart will not be part of that customized tab anymore.
You may choose whether or not to receive notifications from us, in case we identify a new loss-making pattern, when you identify a trade that fits in a loss-making pattern or when we identify a high risk-taking. You can choose the way you wish to receive the notification from us. Additionally, you may select and de-select to receive notifications on specific output charts depending on your preferences.
You may choose to have your trading performance public, thereby allowing other users of the website to see your trading performance and discuss with them. You may choose to only have certain parts of your trading output public, thereby limiting the amount of information publicly presented to other users. We always seek to act in line with your personal privacy preferences, therefore you at any time choose to change to public/ private setting. Please note, for the calculation of the benchmark data, we may use the trading data in an anonymized independent of the public/ private setting.
You may choose to share your trade analytics outputs with the community and your friends. If your trade analytics profile is set as public, other traders will be able to visit your profile and see your charts. Additionally, your trade performance will be part of the trader scoreboard and may be presented in the trader carousel in the community.
Once a trading account is connect, a thread under forum will be automatically generated. Upon clicking the button “Discuss here” on the left top of the dashboard, you will be directed to this specific thread where people can discuss freely about the trading strategy about this trading account.
When analyzing your trades, you can choose to present the data in different timeframes. This will allow you to inspect certain changes in the trading behavior over time. Please keep in mind, that a shorter timeframe may also mean, that the results are calculated on less trades per category, thereby limiting the significance level of the outputs. When choosing a certain timeframe, only the trades opened and closed during this timeframe will be taken as input for the analyses.
